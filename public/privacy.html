Privacy Policy

Last Stop Mail stores information submitted through the availability and reservation forms so we can respond to business inquiries and personally follow up about campaign spots.

What we collect

Submitted forms may include your business name, contact name, email, phone number, website, selected category, pricing plan, offer details, and optional notes.

Why we store it

We use this information to answer availability questions, follow up about interest in a spot, and continue the conversation about creative production and campaign timing.

Local storage for this demo

In this implementation, data is stored on the server in local JSON files. If you want production deployment later, this can be upgraded to a real database and email notification workflow.
