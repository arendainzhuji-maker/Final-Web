Frequently Asked Questions

What mail services do you offer? ∨

We help with direct mail campaigns, postcard design, print coordination, postage, and Canada Post delivery — whether you need one piece of the process or the full stack handled for you.

Do I need to design the piece myself? ∨

No. Design support is included when you work with us on a campaign. You provide your business details, offer, and brand assets — we handle the rest.

What happens when I reach out? ∨

Zhuji will personally send you a message after you submit, and from there you can talk through your mailing needs, timing, and pricing.

Can you handle printing or postage only? ∨

Yes. Some businesses need a full campaign; others need help with printing, postage, or delivery only. Tell us what you need on the form and we will quote accordingly.

Why direct mail instead of more online ads? ∨

Mail lands in the home. For local Edmonton businesses, a physical postcard or mailer creates neighbourhood visibility without competing in crowded digital feeds.
